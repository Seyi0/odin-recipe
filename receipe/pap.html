Pap is a Nigerian breakfast dish made from fermented corn. Hereâ€™s how to prepare it:

  • Soak the corn in water for 2-3 days
  • Blend the soaked corn with water to form a smooth paste
  • Pour the paste into a pot and cook over low heat, stirring constantly
  • Once it thickens, remove from heat and let it cool
  • Serve with sugar, milk, or honey
