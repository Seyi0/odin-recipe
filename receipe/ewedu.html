Ewedu soup is a popular Nigerian dish made from jute leaves. Hereâ€™s how to prepare it:

  • Wash and chop the ewedu leaves
  • Blend the leaves with a little water until smooth
  • Pour the blended leaves into a pot and cook over medium heat
  • Add ground crayfish, salt, and seasoning cubes to taste
  • Cook for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  • Serve hot with amala, fufu, or any swallow of your choice
